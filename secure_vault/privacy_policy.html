Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026 | Developer: Analogte

Summary - Your Privacy is Protected

  • We do NOT collect your personal data
  • We do NOT have access to your files or passwords
  • All encryption happens on your device
  • Your data never leaves your device

1. Information Collection

1.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your personal information
  • We do NOT collect your files, photos, or documents
  • We do NOT collect your passwords or encryption keys
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services

1.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Encrypted files Your secured files and photos Device storage
Vault metadata Manage your vaults Local database
Encryption keys Decrypt your files Secure storage
App settings Your preferences Local storage

1.3 Biometric Data

If you enable biometric authentication (fingerprint/face), biometric data is processed entirely by your device's operating system. We never access, store, or transmit your biometric information.

2. How Your Data is Protected

2.1 Client-Side Encryption

All encryption and decryption happens exclusively on your device:

  • Encryption Algorithm: AES-256-GCM (military-grade)
  • Key Derivation: PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations
  • Your password: Never stored, only used to derive encryption keys

2.2 Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We operate on a zero-knowledge principle:

  • We cannot decrypt your files
  • We cannot recover your password
  • We cannot access your data even if legally requested (we don't have it)

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

4. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is on your device
  • Delete: Delete any file or vault at any time
  • Export: Export your decrypted files whenever you want
  • Uninstall: Removing the app deletes all app data
Important: We cannot recover your password. If you forget your password, your encrypted data cannot be recovered. This is by design to ensure maximum security.

5. Data Retention

  • While using the app: Data is stored locally on your device
  • Deleted files: Moved to trash, permanently deleted after 30 days
  • After uninstalling: All app data is removed from your device

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any personal information from any users, this naturally extends to children as well.

7. Security Measures

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for all files
  • PBKDF2 key derivation with high iteration count
  • Secure storage for encryption keys
  • Auto-lock feature
  • Screenshot prevention option
  • Biometric authentication support

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

9. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with GDPR, CCPA, PDPA (Thailand), and Google Play Store requirements.
